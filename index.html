🤖 WHAT IS AI PHISHING DETECTION?

📌 Understanding Phishing

Phishing is a cyber attack where hackers create fake websites that look exactly like real ones (banks, social media, shopping sites) to steal your passwords, credit card details, and personal information.

🧠 How AI Detection Works

  • URL Pattern Recognition: AI analyzes URL structure, length, special characters, and suspicious keywords
  • Behavioral Analysis: Examines website behavior, redirects, and hidden iframes
  • SSL Certificate Check: Verifies if the website has valid encryption
  • Domain Age & Reputation: Checks how old the domain is and its history
  • Malware Signature Detection: Scans for known malware patterns and malicious code
  • Real-time Threat Intelligence: Cross-references with global phishing databases
